Apps

Five tools, one subscription

Every app is a native desktop window launched from the nOffice shell. They share a local AI assistant and support all standard file formats.

nWrite

Word processor with tables, images, footnotes, comments, and track changes. Export to PDF, DOCX, HTML.

nSheet

Spreadsheet with HyperFormula engine and WebGL grid. Charts, pivot tables, millions of rows. Import and export CSV, XLSX, ODS.

nSlides

Presentations with transitions, animations, and speaker notes. Export to PDF and PPTX. Present directly from the app.

nImg

Image editor with layers, brushes, shapes, and filters. Generative AI for txt2img, img2img, inpainting, outpainting.

nCode

IDE with Monaco Editor. LSP support for Python, Rust, Go, C++, Ruby. Git integration, multi-root workspaces.

Built-in AI Assistant

Every app has an AI sidebar. Chat, rewrite, summarize, translate, generate, explain. Runs locally on a Qwen3 8B model. Fully offline.

A Qwen3 8B model runs on your machine via llama.cpp. 32K context window, GPU accelerated. Your data never leaves your computer.
